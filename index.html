About

PhD in Applied Mathematics

Hello, I hold a PhD in Applied Mathematics from the University of Caen-Normandy/France, where I worked under the supervision of Prof. Jalal Fadili at the GREYC laboratory.
As an applied mathematician, I dedicate part of my research to solving complex inverse problems as well as optimization algorithms.
My recent work focuses on phase reconstruction using non-Euclidean Bregman-type optimization algorithms.
Additionally, I am interested in optimal transport, particularly in its Eulerian formulation and its numerical resolution through partial differential equation methods. Beyond my research, I am committed to promoting science and am attentive to issues related to research funding and organization.

Areas of Interest : Optimization (convex, non-convex/smooth, non-smooth), Bregman-based algorithms, solving inverse problems/phase reconstruction, and computational optimal transport.
